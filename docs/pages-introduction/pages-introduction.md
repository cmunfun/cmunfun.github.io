# UNFUN AI+

**UNFUN AI+** is a personal knowledge repository focused on **AI+, Strategic Management, Digital Transformation, IPD, Project Management, Digital Marketing, Integrated Supply Chain, and Intelligent Manufacturing**.

**UNFUN AI+** 是一个聚焦 **AI+、战略管理、数字化转型、IPD、项目管理、数字化营销、集成供应链与智能制造** 的个人知识仓库。

## Overview / 概览

This repository is used to organize knowledge, notes, frameworks, and practical reflections related to enterprise transformation and AI-enabled business operations.

该仓库用于整理企业转型与 AI 赋能业务运营相关的知识、笔记、框架与实践思考。

The content focuses on business scenarios, management models, process logic, data-driven decision-making, and digital system thinking.

内容重点关注业务场景、管理模型、流程逻辑、数据驱动决策与数字化系统思维。
