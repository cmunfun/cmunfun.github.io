# Supply Chain

Integrated Supply Chain focuses on demand, planning, procurement, manufacturing, inventory, logistics, delivery, and end-to-end collaboration.

集成供应链聚焦需求、计划、采购、制造、库存、物流、交付与端到端协同。

## Topics

- Demand Management
- Planning
- Procurement
- Manufacturing Operations
- Inventory Management
- Logistics and Delivery
- End-to-end Collaboration
